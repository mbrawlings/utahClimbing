Our top 5 picks for Utah sport climbing areas

  • American Fork Canyon

    • Quality of climbing: 5/5

    Rock Quality: 3/5

    Quantity: 5/5

    Accessibility: 5/5

    Most climbers would agree that American Fork Canyon isn't the prettiest rock to climb on. The atmosphere also isn't the top of it's class. However, the sheer quantity of developed routes helps it make our top 5. The history behind this canyon is riveting. It comes a unique, powerful style that keeps climbers coming back for more.

  • Maple Canyon

    • Quality of climbing: 5/5

    Rock Quality: 4/5

    Quantity: 5/5

    Accessibility: 3/5

    Definitely the most iconic and well-known bouldering area in Utah. Joes Valley has over 700 established climbs and more are developed each year. Climbers travel from all over both the country and the world to climb on this gorgeous sandstone.
